Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed to store and manage client information securely. We are committed to ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and is protected against unauthorized access.

Data Collection and Use

The app collects only the data you provide, such as client names, contact information, and fitness program details, for the purpose of managing your client database. This data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Data Security

We implement basic security measures to safeguard the data stored in the app. However, users are responsible for ensuring their devices are secure to prevent unauthorized access to the app.

User Control

You have full control over the data in the app. You can add, update, or delete client information at any time. The app does not retain deleted data.

Internet Connectivity

This app operates offline and does not transmit or store data on external servers or online platforms.

Help

This app is designed for personal trainers to efficiently manage their client database. With this tool, you can keep track and manage client details.

Features & How to Use Them

  1. Add A Client

    To add a new client, click the "Add Client" button and fill out the required fields, such as name, date of birth, gender, fitness program, and contact details. Once completed, click "Add Client."

  2. Edit Client Details

    Click the "Edit" button within the client's information to update their details. Make the necessary changes and save them.

  3. Delete A Client

    To remove a client, click the "Delete" button within the client's information (underneath the 'Edit' button). A confirmation message will appear to ensure no accidental deletions.

  4. Set VIP Clients

    Mark your most important clients as VIPs by toggling the VIP option in their details. This helps you prioritize them in your workflow.

  5. Search For Clients

    Use the search bar to quickly find specific clients by their ID number. This feature saves time when managing a large client list.

  6. Sort Clients

    Organize your client list by sorting them based on ID, name, join date, or other criteria.
